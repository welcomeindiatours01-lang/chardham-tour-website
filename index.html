About Char Dham Yatra

The Char Dham Yatra is one of the most sacred pilgrimages in Hinduism, comprising four holy sites in the Himalayas: Yamunotri, Gangotri, Kedarnath, and Badrinath. This spiritual journey takes you through breathtaking landscapes and ancient temples, offering a unique blend of devotion and natural beauty.

Our carefully curated tour packages ensure a comfortable and memorable pilgrimage experience with expert guides, quality accommodation, and complete travel arrangements.

The Four Sacred Dhams

Yamunotri

The source of the Yamuna River, dedicated to Goddess Yamuna. Located at an altitude of 3,293 meters.

Gangotri

The origin of the holy Ganges River, dedicated to Goddess Ganga. Situated at 3,100 meters above sea level.

Kedarnath

One of the twelve Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva, located at 3,583 meters in the Garhwal Himalayas.

Badrinath

Dedicated to Lord Vishnu, one of the Char Dhams. Located at 3,300 meters in the Chamoli district.
